The Bully

Inspirational Quotes/Advice for those who want to change


  1. It takes much more energy to be mean than it does to be nice.

  2. Someone loves you very much, or will love you very much in the future. Why risk losing love?

  3. If you are leaving a digital footprint of bullying online, you are setting yourself up for great failure in the future.

  4. Odds are, the person you are bullying may be your boss 20 years down the road.

  5. All of the negative energy you give out will attract the same toward you in your own life.

  6. Read about compassion, empathy and energy.

    7. Real life example of bullies

    I started being a bully 1 year ago when this girl in my tutor group went and got her hair cut really short and I did not like it. In a way it look like lady gaga so I called her it then I started hitting her and being mean to her. At break and lunch she would come up to me and say so then shall we get this over and done with, and a week came to two week then to three weeks and I started being really mean and then one day I followed her home and after that I watched her getting changed in the changing room. Then I thought to myself and said why I am doing this just because she has short hair, so I stopped. Now we are friends. I have stopped bullying because you get nothing out of it, you are not pushing others out the way you are pushing yourself away. You lose friends bullying and you just get yourself in trouble.
